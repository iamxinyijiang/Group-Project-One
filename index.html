⭐Simple To-do List⭐

A simple To-do List manager for you and your group.

*Task Name must not be empty or longer than 8 characters.
*Must not be blank;
*Maximum length: 15 characters.
*Must select at least one person.
*Due date must not be empty.
*Due date must be set in the future.
*Status must be selected.

⬇ Once added, tasks will appear here.⬇
